Getting started
Importing books
Tobi supports EPUB and PDF files that are DRM-free. Most ebooks you buy from Kindle or Apple Books have DRM protection and cannot be imported — the file will appear to open but Tobi won't be able to read the text inside.
Recommended: Use the built-in Get Books tab to download from Project Gutenberg — 70,000 public domain classics, always DRM-free.
Books purchased from Kindle, Apple Books, Kobo, and most major retailers include DRM (Digital Rights Management) copy protection. This prevents any app other than the official reader from accessing the text — including Tobi.

DRM-free alternatives:
  • Standard Ebooks — free, beautifully formatted public domain books
  • Smashwords — indie authors who sell DRM-free
  • Humble Bundle — frequent book bundles, always DRM-free
  • Tor Books — major sci-fi/fantasy publisher, DRM-free
Several ways work well:
  • AirDrop from Mac: Right-click the EPUB file on your Mac → Share → AirDrop → your iPad. The file arrives in Files → On My iPad → Downloads.
  • iCloud Drive: Save the file to your iCloud Drive on any device. It syncs to your iPad automatically. Open Tobi → Import → Browse Files → iCloud Drive.
  • Email attachment: Email the EPUB to yourself, open the attachment on iPad, tap Share → Copy to Tobi.
  • Safari download: Download directly in Safari. The file appears in the Downloads folder in Files.
Once the file is anywhere in the Files app, tap Import in Tobi's Library screen to browse to it.
Yes — Tobi registers as a handler for EPUB and PDF files on your iPad. From any app that supports sharing (Safari, Mail, Files, Dropbox, Google Drive), tap the Share button, then tap Open in Tobi. The book will be imported automatically and you'll see a confirmation.
Note: If "Open in Tobi" doesn't appear, scroll right in the share sheet to find it, or tap "More" to enable it.
Long-press any book card in your Library to reveal the delete option. This removes it from your library and frees the storage space on your device. Generated panels are unaffected — if you've already converted the book, the conversion is still available.
Generation
Converting
Chapter 1 is typically ready within 60–90 seconds. You'll receive a notification when it's ready to read. The remaining chapters generate in the background while you read — by the time you finish chapter 1, chapter 2 is usually already done.

Total conversion time for a full novel is typically 8–15 minutes, but you don't need to wait for it — reading starts at 90 seconds.
Yes. The style input field accepts any text description you can imagine. Try things like:
  • "Watercolour with ink outlines, Studio Ghibli palette"
  • "Soviet propaganda poster style, bold geometric shapes"
  • "Ukiyo-e woodblock print, traditional Japanese colours"
  • "80s Heavy Metal magazine illustration, detailed crosshatching"
The more specific you are, the more consistent the results. The preset styles are just starting points — the real power is in the freeform field.
Yes — up to 5 different styles per book on the Pro and Lifetime plans. Each style creates a separate graphic novel version in your library. The free tier allows 1 style for your 1 free conversion.
You can navigate away from the progress screen at any time — generation continues in the background. To fully stop a conversion in progress, force-quit the app. Note that any panels already generated will be lost and the conversion will need to be restarted.
Monthly panel budgets reset on the 1st of each calendar month. You can see how many panels you have remaining this month in Settings → Subscription.

Pro subscribers get 160 panels/month (~2 full books). Lifetime subscribers get 600 panels/month (~7 full books). As the community cache grows over time, cached panels don't reduce your budget — so in practice you'll get more books per month as the app matures.
Output quality
Panels & quality
Tobi builds a character consistency profile from the full text of your book before generating any panels. For most books this works very well — characters maintain consistent faces, hair, and clothing across the conversion.

Occasional variation can happen when:
  • A character appears very rarely and is described vaguely in the source text
  • The book has a very large cast of secondary characters
  • The source PDF was scanned or image-based (text extraction is limited)
If you notice significant inconsistency, try converting again — the AI generation process has natural variation and a second attempt often produces better results.
Blank panels usually indicate that a scene was too short or abstract for the AI to generate a meaningful image from — philosophical passages, internal monologue, or very short transitional scenes sometimes produce this. It is also occasionally caused by a temporary API timeout during generation.

To fix: long-press the book card → Regenerate. This will only regenerate the missing panels, not the whole book.
Tobi reads the text layer of PDF files. If your PDF is a scanned image (a photo of a physical book), there is no text layer and Tobi cannot extract the content.

Check your PDF: try selecting text in it. If you can't select text, it's image-based and Tobi cannot convert it. EPUB format almost always works better — if a book is available in both formats, choose EPUB.
The biggest impact comes from being more specific in your style description. Instead of "manga style", try "black and white manga with heavy ink lines, dramatic shadows, close-up character expressions, cinematic panel composition". The AI responds very well to detailed, specific prompts.

You can also try the Berserk skin for darker, more detailed linework, or the Sakura skin for softer, more colourful output.
Billing
Subscriptions
All subscriptions are managed by Apple — Tobi doesn't handle billing directly. To cancel:
  1. Open Settings on your iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Tap Tobi Pro
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription
Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You keep Pro access until then — no partial refunds are issued for unused time.
Refunds are handled by Apple directly. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the Tobi purchase, and submit a refund request. Apple typically responds within 48 hours.

If you have a billing concern you'd like us to be aware of, please contact us below and we'll do our best to help.
Open Tobi on your new iPad, go to Settings, and tap Restore Purchases. Sign in with the same Apple ID you used when you originally purchased and your Pro subscription or Lifetime plan will be restored automatically.
Pro ($4.99/month) gives you 160 panels/month and all skins unlocked, billed monthly and cancelable at any time.

Lifetime ($29.99 once) gives you 600 panels/month and all skins, forever, with a single one-time payment. It pays for itself after 6 months of Pro.

Both tiers benefit from the community cache — as more users generate panels, your effective output per month increases because cached panels don't count against your generation budget.
Troubleshooting
Technical issues
Try these steps in order:
  1. Force-quit Tobi and reopen it (swipe up from the bottom, swipe Tobi off screen)
  2. Check for an app update in the App Store
  3. Restart your iPad (hold power + volume button)
  4. Check that you have at least 1GB of free storage (Settings → General → iPad Storage)
If the crash is reproducible, please contact us below with details of what you were doing when it occurred — this helps us fix it quickly.
Your library and already-converted graphic novels are available offline — you can read them without an internet connection. Converting a new book requires internet as it uses AI services to generate the panels.
The app itself is small (~50MB). Each converted graphic novel takes approximately 15–20MB depending on the number of chapters and the image quality of the selected style. A library of 10 converted books uses roughly 150–200MB total.
Check that notifications are enabled for Tobi:
  1. Open Settings on your iPad
  2. Scroll down and tap Tobi
  3. Tap Notifications and make sure they're enabled
If notifications are already enabled and still not arriving, force-quit Tobi and reopen it, then try a new conversion.
